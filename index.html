Why use an immersion techinque?

The point of an immersion approach is to learn the target language in a similar fashion to how a child would pick up their first language. This method is meant for the brain to build a natural intuition in the languange, so that when one is learning to speak they have an almost inate understanding of what sounds right and what sounds wrong.

How long will it take to learn my target language using an immersion approach?

It all depends on a few things such as:

    How similar your target language is to your native language
    How long you spend with your language per day
    The time it takes for you to get familiar with the content you studied
    How well you adjust to your immersion content to get progressively more complicated

There are 3 main components to the immersion tecnique.

    (ON LEVEL)Audiocontent to get used to the spoken language
    (On level)Reading material to get used to the visual language
    A spacial repition system used to increas vocabulary
